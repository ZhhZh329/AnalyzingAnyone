In a 2023 interview with Lex Fridman, when asked "Are you happy?", Musk gave a long pause before saying: "There are many nights I sleep alone." He has had multiple marriages (twice to actress Talulah Riley) and has fathered at least 12 children with multiple partners. His first child, Nevada, died at 10 weeks from sudden infant death syndrome in 2002. When asked about the tragedy, he reportedly returned to work almost immediately, telling a colleague: "I'm not sure what sitting around being sad is going to do."
