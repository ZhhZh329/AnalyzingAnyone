Musk has been one of the most vocal critics of unregulated AI development. In 2014 he said: "With artificial intelligence we are summoning the demon. You know all those stories where there's the guy with the pentagram and the holy water, and he's like, yeah, he's sure he can control the demon? Doesn't work out." He co-founded OpenAI in 2015 as a nonprofit to develop "friendly AI," but later departed the board in 2018 citing conflicts of interest with Tesla's AI work. He subsequently sued OpenAI in 2024, alleging the company had abandoned its open-source, nonprofit mission. In 2023, he founded xAI as a competitor.
Source: MIT symposium (2014), various interviews
