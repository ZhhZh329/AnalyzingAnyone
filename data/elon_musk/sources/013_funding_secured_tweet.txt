On August 7, 2018, Musk tweeted: "Am considering taking Tesla private at $420. Funding secured." The SEC sued Musk for securities fraud, alleging the tweet was "false and misleading." The $420 price was widely interpreted as a reference to cannabis culture. According to reporting, when Tesla's board initially rejected the SEC's proposed settlement, Musk had threatened to resign. The resulting stock crash forced acceptance of a harsher deal: Musk paid a $20 million fine and agreed to have his tweets pre-approved by a lawyer.
Source: SEC filings, Wall Street Journal
