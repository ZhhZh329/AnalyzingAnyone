In a 2020 interview with Joe Rogan, Musk said: "I think we have a duty to maintain the light of consciousness, to make sure it continues into the future." When pressed on what motivates him, he said: "I'm not trying to be anyone's savior. I'm just trying to think about the future and not be sad." He has also described his personal philosophy: "What I'm trying to do is to maximize the probability of the future being better."
Source: Joe Rogan Experience (2020), various interviews
