Walter Isaacson's biography describes what colleagues call Musk's "demon mode" — periods of intense, destructive energy where he becomes fixated on gutting something. Isaacson traces this pattern to Musk's abusive childhood with his father. "When Elon is in demon mode, he will fire people, destroy entire teams' work, demand impossible deadlines — and then somehow the product ships. The question is whether the destruction was necessary for the result, or whether the result could have been achieved another way."
Source: Walter Isaacson biography (2023)
