Two days before Christmas 2008, NASA awarded SpaceX a $1.6 billion contract for 12 cargo transport flights to the International Space Station. This came at a time when both SpaceX and Tesla were nearly bankrupt. Musk has described the week between the fourth Falcon 1 success and the NASA contract as one of the most stressful periods of his life. "I had to borrow money from friends just to pay rent."
Source: Ashlee Vance biography, NASA press releases
