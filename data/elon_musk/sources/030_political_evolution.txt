Musk has described his political evolution: "I used to be a moderate Democrat. I didn't leave the Democrats. The Democrats left me." In 2024 he became the largest individual political donor in U.S. history, giving $288 million to Republican candidates through his America PAC. He publicly endorsed Trump after an assassination attempt on Trump in July 2024. He spoke at campaign rallies and ran a controversial sweepstakes offering $1 million daily to registered voters in swing states, which prompted lawsuits alleging he was illegally paying people to vote.
Source: FEC filings, various news reports
