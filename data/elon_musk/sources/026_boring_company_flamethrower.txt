In 2017, Musk founded The Boring Company to build underground tunnels for traffic. To fund it, in early 2018 he sold 20,000 branded flamethrowers at $500 each, raising $10 million. When asked about the logic of a tunnel company selling flamethrowers, he said it was "obviously a terrible idea" but noted they sold out immediately. His approach to marketing often combines ironic humor with genuine products — he once sold 50,000 Boring Company-branded hats before the company had dug a single tunnel.
