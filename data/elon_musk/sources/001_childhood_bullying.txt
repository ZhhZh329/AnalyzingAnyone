Growing up in Pretoria, South Africa, Elon Musk was severely bullied at school. He was once thrown down a flight of stairs by classmates and beaten so badly he was hospitalized. He later described himself as "the youngest and smallest kid in class" who was "a prime target." His father Errol Musk was emotionally abusive; Elon has publicly called him "a terrible human being" and said living with him was "not a happy childhood." Despite this, after his parents' divorce, young Elon chose to live with his father — a decision he later called "a mistake."
Source: Ashlee Vance biography (2015), Walter Isaacson biography (2023)
