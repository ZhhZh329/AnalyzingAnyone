At the November 2023 DealBook Summit, when confronted about advertisers leaving the platform due to content moderation concerns, Musk responded: "If someone's going to try to blackmail me with advertising, blackmail me with money? Go fuck yourself." He then added: "Is that clear? I hope it is." The statement caused a further exodus of advertisers. By 2024, the Wall Street Journal described the Twitter acquisition as "the worst deal in merger finance since the 2008 financial crisis," with the company having lost an estimated 79% of its equity value.
