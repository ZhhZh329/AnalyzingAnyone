In January 2025, Musk was designated a "special government employee" leading the Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE) in Trump's second administration. He applied his corporate approach to government — mass layoffs, system shutdowns, and rapid restructuring of federal agencies. He was criticized for his treatment of federal employees and his approach to cutting government programs. In May 2025, he stepped down from the role, stating: "The DOGE mission will only strengthen over time as it becomes a way of life throughout the government." A federal judge ruled that Musk had acted as the "de facto leader" of DOGE.
Source: Various news reports, TIME (2025)
