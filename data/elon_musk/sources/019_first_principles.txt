Musk frequently discusses "first principles thinking" as his core problem-solving method. In a 2013 TED interview he explained: "I tend to approach things from a physics framework. Physics teaches you to reason from first principles rather than by analogy. So I said, okay, what are the fundamental truths of this problem, and reason up from there. This is what SpaceX is based on — not saying 'rockets have always been expensive, so they always will be,' but asking 'what are rockets actually made of? What's the raw material cost?'"
Source: TED Talk 2013
