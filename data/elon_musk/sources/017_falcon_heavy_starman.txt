On February 6, 2018, SpaceX successfully launched the Falcon Heavy — the most powerful operational rocket in the world at the time. As its test payload, Musk sent his personal midnight-cherry Tesla Roadster into space with a mannequin dubbed "Starman" in the driver's seat, playing David Bowie's "Space Oddity" on loop. He later said: "I love the thought of a car drifting apparently endlessly through space and perhaps being discovered by an alien race millions of years in the future."
Source: SpaceX webcast, multiple interviews
