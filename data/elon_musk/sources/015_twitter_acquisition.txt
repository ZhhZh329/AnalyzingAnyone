"the bird is freed" — Elon Musk, October 28, 2022, upon completing his $44 billion acquisition of Twitter.

In the days following the acquisition, Musk laid off approximately 80% of Twitter's workforce, including most of middle management. He personally reviewed code commits to determine who was productive. He sent an email giving remaining employees an ultimatum: commit to "extremely hardcore" work or leave with severance. Approximately 1,200 additional employees resigned after the email.
Source: Various news reports, internal email leaks
