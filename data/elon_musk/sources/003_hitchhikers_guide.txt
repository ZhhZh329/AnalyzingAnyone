Musk has repeatedly cited Douglas Adams' "The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy" as a formative influence. In interviews he has said: "I was around 12 or 15 when I had an existential crisis. I was reading various books on philosophy and religion and I was trying to figure out the meaning of life. Then I read Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy and it pointed out that the real challenge is figuring out what questions to ask. Once you can properly phrase the question, the answer is the easy part."
Source: Multiple interviews, including TED 2017
